Sr. Magento Developer
20/164-TEC-SR-DEV
Required Experience
Required Experience & Skills:
  • In-depth knowledge of Magento 2's code structure, extension architecture, theming hierarchy, and fallback components and must have worked for at least 2 end-to-end eCommerce projects.
  • Building a Magento site from an approved design to a functional storefront.
  • Development of new modules and extensions creation.
  • Good programming skills in Magento including theme & plugins.
  • Strong knowledge of OOPs.
  • Ability to integrate Magento2 Theme & Custom Module Development.
  • Manage live e-commerce websites and their social integration.
  • Good understanding of Web Analytics and SEO techniques
  • Proficient in PHP, MySQL OPS, XML, XHTML, CSS, JavaScript, Modules i.e. API integration, Payment Gateways, XML with a focus on standards
  • Experience in creating applications using a framework such as CodeIgniter, LAMP, Zend, Yii, Laravel, etc.
  • Understanding of modern UI/UX trends.
  • Ability to project-manage and work to strict deadlines.
  • Effective communication skills, self-motivated, team player and ability to solve complex problems independently.
Role and Responsibilities:
  • Full lifecycle application development to deploy e-commerce stores.
  • Work on Magento2 integration with external systems viz CMS/ CRM / ERP/ Payment/ Shipping.
  • Building and configuring Magento 2x eCommerce websites, Coding Magento templates.
  • Understand client requirements, translate them into techno-functional requirements, HLD & LLD,
  • Setting performance tasks and goals, troubleshooting integration issues and Updating website features and security patches.
  • Adhere to project plan and highlight risks/issues in a timely manner.
  • Provide production support to e-commerce websites, integration with middle/back office systems.
  • Enhancing skill on new technologies, frameworks, and methodologies.
  • Follow latest trends on Magento, analyze fitment and propose implementation techniques.
  • Coordination with Customer Success Manager (CSM) regarding project updates, risks & issues.
  • Working closely with founders to establish best practices.
  • Candidate would be required to lead and guide the junior/mid-level developers over their implementations, integrate their works and maintain integrity of application and its architecture
  • The candidate should be able to work without supervision to complete project task and must possess initiative to address issues and opportunities.
Skills & Qualification:
  • BE/Btech/BCA/MCA/ BIT or equivalent full-time degree
  • Magento certified developer (preferred)
Perks/Benefits:
  • Attractive career path – no limits on growth for the right individual
  • Great location – just 10 mins away from Golf Course Road underpass.
