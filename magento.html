Magento Developer
20/163-TEC-DEV
Required Experience
Are you a self-motivated positive thinker and a quick learner with a ‘can do’ attitude? If yes, then we like to meet you… Here is a great opportunity for you to be a part of a rock star team! Look for the career opportunities below and share your resume with careers@velocitai.com
Required Experience & Skills:
  • Must have minimum 3 years of experience working on Magento Projects with In-depth knowledge of Magento 2's code structure, extension architecture, theming hierarchy, and fallback components.
  • Experience with Magento eCommerce Community and Enterprise editions versions 1.9 - 2.x.
  • Experience on at least 2 end-to-end eCommerce projects.
  • Proficient in PHP, MySQL OPS, XML, XHTML, CSS, JavaScript, Modules i.e. API integration, Payment Gateways, XML with a focus on standards.
  • Ability to integrate Magento2 Theme & Custom Module Development.
  • Managed live e-commerce websites and their social integration.
  • Good understanding of Web Analytics and SEO techniques
  • Experience in creating applications using a framework such as CodeIgniter, LAMP, Zend, Yii, Laravel, etc.
  • Strong Working knowledge of shopping cart development with shipping, and Payment Gateway Integration.
  • Should have strong knowledge of OOPS, MVC architecture.
  • Should have experience in building highly interactive, high traffic websites and database driven web applications.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve bugs found in existing Magento Installations possess initiative to address issues and opportunities.
  • Effective communication skills, self-motivated, and a team player.
Role and Responsibilities:
  • Full lifecycle application development to deploy e-commerce stores.
  • Work on Magento2 integration with external systems viz CMS/ CRM / ERP/ Payment/ Shipping etc.
  • Understand client requirements, translate them into techno-functional requirements, HLD & LLD
  • Adhere to project plan and highlight risks/issues in a timely manner.
  • Provide production support to e-commerce websites, integration with middle/back office systems.
  • Enhancing skill on new technologies, frameworks, and methodologies.
  • Follow latest trends on Magento, analyze fitment and propose implementation techniques.
  • The candidate should be able to work without supervision to complete project task and must
Skills & Qualification:
  • BE/Btech/BCA/MCA/ BIT or equivalent full-time degree
  • Magento certified developer (preferred)
Perks/Benefits:
  • Attractive career path – no limits on growth for the right individual
  • Great location – just 10 mins away from Golf Course Road underpass.
