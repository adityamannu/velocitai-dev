COVID impact on Direct-to-Consumer eRetailers

COVID-19 is nothing short of a black swan event. With cases towering over 9.9 million cases globally, and fatalities in excess of five hundred thousand, countries seem to be scampering to find a way to arrest the spread. As high street stores retreated into lockdown across the world, eCommerce was propelled to an unprecedented growth. With this, D2C eRetailers have before themselves, a massive opportunity, that needs careful navigation around a novel set of changing world order.
