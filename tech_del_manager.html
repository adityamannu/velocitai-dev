Technical Delivery Manager
20/141-TEC-MGRV
Required Experience
Required Experience & Skills:
  • 8-12 years of strong design & development of multi-website multi-store eCommerce applications.
  • At least 6 years on core platforms – Magento 2.x, Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce
  • Qualification: B.E/B.Tech/M.E/M.Tech/MCA (preferably in Computer Science or Information Technology)
  • Job Clincher: Magento 2 certification + Strong Project Management + Python
  • Mandatory skill set: Magento 2, LAMP stack, PHP, HTML, CSS, JSON, XML, REST API, JS, Jquery, React JS, Angular JS, Node JS, MySQL, Shopify
  • Added advantage skills: Python, AWS deployment and configuration, MongoDB, Computer Vision
  • Key behavioural traits: thinks like an entrepreneur, strong CAN-DO attitude, highly driven, passionate technologist looking for an opportunity to build disruptive products
  • Expertise in Magento’s technical architecture, Continuous Integration
  • Solid understanding of Magento fundamentals, LAMP stack, MVC, OOPS & design patterns
  • Experience with at least one MVC frameworks like Zend, CakePHP, Codeignitor.
  • Strong database architecture skills, MySQL knowledge, experience interacting with database systems through abstraction layer
  • Must have knowledge of code versioning (SVN/GIT)
  • Experience in identifying and implementing performance optimizations, understanding scalability concepts
  • Proficiency in 3rd party integration with ERPs, payment gateways, logistics tracking system etc.
  • In-depth knowledge of web-application security vulnerabilities and how to avoid them, understanding PCI compliance concepts
  • Attention to detail, and ability to multitask and meet deadlines
  • Passion for quality and engineering excellence at scale
  • Demonstrated ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously, meet deadlines, effectively manage priorities and communicate progress
Role and Responsibilities:
  • Lead technology delivery of webstores, extensions, APIs, themes as per specifications
  • Create detailed application architecture specifications that outline the various layers and common components to be reused by application developers.
  • Responsible for meeting strict performance & page load time targets, collaborate with DevOps to strategize adequate infrastructure footprint with a low TCO
  • Develop custom themes, extensions, APIs and utilities
  • Conceptualize and experiment with alternative architectural approaches
  • Define and participate in the code review process to ensure that the code conforms to and Magento best practices and guidelines.
  • Articulates the architectural vision to clients, sponsors and stakeholders
  • Assist in estimating the size and scope of projects on the e-commerce B2C roadmap.
  • Implement agile and fit-for-purpose project management tools, and present delivery updates to management
  • Escalate delivery risks, identify & propose mitigation strategies in a timely manner
  • Validates clients roadmap against Magento current or expected features
  • Mentor and lead team members of various experience levels
  • Ownership of overall architecture, design, development and production support for eCommerce book of work
  • Thought leadership & contribution to roadmap of the firm, and scaling technology operations
  • Define and implement delivery best practices, project management tools
  • Responsible for lights-on & production support activities
  • Coordinate with DevOps on infrastructure activities, server administration, environment management, and ensure DevOps best practices are in place
Perks/Benefits:
  • Attractive career path – no limits on growth for the right individual
  • Participation in ESOP program
  • Great location – just 10 mins away from Golf Course Road underpass
  • Oh man, I have to work on my birthday? NO. you get off!
  • Informal, relaxed dress code
